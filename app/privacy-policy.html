What We Collect

Our policy refers to the personal information that is collected here on our website or gathered through our predetermined methods (and the methods we’ll dive deeper into below). We’ve put forth this policy in order to help you best understand the type of information we will collect, what type of information we do not collect, and how you can best manage your privacy. It is important to note that this privacy policy doesn’t pertain to or apply to the information that we receive from customers in our present role. We’d be happy to expound on this point further, as we will in our outlined information below.

Further, upon visiting our site, you’ll notice that we request more information from you that you have the capability to agree with or deny. For example, some options we submit are things like email newsletters or downloadable content, these will likely require forms that require you to submit information that is personal (for example, name, email, company name, title, etc.). This personal information is gathered with your consent only, and it’s likely we will group this in with the other information we’ve gleaned from you based on our cookies technology. Beyond this, we’ll likely send you information (with your consent) that could gather more of your private information, such as surveys and emails.

So, why exactly do we collect all of this information? To better understand our clients and audience! By using cookies to scrape information, we are better able to improve our website as well as adapt its functionality so it better serves the people who view it and use it. It’s also a great way for us to monitor fraudulent activity and better understand how people get to our website, what they’re looking for, how long they spend on our website, and more. All of this information is neatly compiled and analyzed to help us create a better user experience for you and our other clients or website visitors.

Further, we will potentially use that information we’ve gathered to better understand our customers – who they are, what they need, and how we can best service them. We also use this information in order to properly contact you with information that we believe will benefit you, to respond to your requests, to send over administrative information, and to hep further your personalized experience on our site. As we stated above, it’s also a helpful tool that aids us in monitoring potential breaches of security, fraudulent behavior, and criminal cyber activity.

Typically, we will only collect personal data where the data will benefit our legitimate interests without violating your rights, where we have the consent to do so, and where the personal information is necessary in order to fulfill a contract with you.

We Protect It

We think this is incredibly important to come right out and say – we do not sell or license your information about how you use our website to anyone. Any third-party that receives your information has agreed to the exact same parameters of information confidentiality and is licensed to use information solely for the purposes we explained above. We will, whenever possible, maintain your confidentiality agreements from any entity whom is disclosed your personal information. However, we do retain the knowledge that in the event of some type of merger or acquisition, the possession of your information could be transferred to the successor.

We do take all of the necessary, legal, and rational steps in order to protect your personal information, but it’s important to understand that when sharing personal information online, you assume several risks. Please note that we will do everything in our power to protect your information, including maintaining appropriate methods to ensure personal data in our organization is kept organized, secure, and inaccessible by unauthorized access.

pIf for some reason you feel as though your interaction with our website is no longer secure, we think it’s important you let us know. Please reach out and contact us in order to alert us to these issues.

You Can Help Manage It

You are able to access your information, as well as change it, delete it, or even selectively limit it. All we ask is that you simply reach out to us to discuss and take care of these issues for you.

Your Privacy Options

Though we do not request that you send over personal information when browsing our site, it’s possible that some of our pages may include options for submitting this kind of data. You have every right and option to ignore these submissions. You are also able to opt-out of emails and administrative messages if you’d like.
